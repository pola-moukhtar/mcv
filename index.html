Recipe overview

This classic Italian pasta dish is rich, creamy, and full of flavor.
It takes only 20 minutes to prepare and is perfect for a quick meal. (Serves 2 people)

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • salt and black pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. cook pancetta until crispy.
  3. mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. combine everything with drained pasta while hot.
  5. season with salt and pepper.
Tips

make sure the pasta is hot when you mix in the egg and cheese mixture for a creamy texture.
